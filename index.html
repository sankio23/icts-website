International Center for Transdisciplinary Studies

The International Center for Transdisciplinary Studies (ICTS) is an inter-university research center that promotes a relational transdisciplinary approach to the study of complex contemporary challenges. By bringing together scholars, institutions, and diverse fields of knowledge, ICTS advances research, education, and international scientific cooperation.

We study what becomes visible when different perspectives are brought into relation.

Disciplines remain essential. Transdisciplinarity, for us, is a way of working when no single framework is sufficient — when a phenomenon can only be described by holding several approaches together, without flattening the differences between them.

To think transdisciplinarily is to refuse the false choice between fragmentation and uniformity. It is to seek complex unity — a unity that does not erase difference but orders it.
