§ Join · Collaborate

Three ways to join the Center.

Affiliation with ICTS is structured through three application modules — for individual researchers, for institutions, and for members proposing a new research structure. Each request is reviewed by the Director and the relevant body of the Center, pursuant to the Center's Regulations.

Module A · For Researchers

Application for admission.

For individual scholars — professors, researchers, postdoctoral fellows, and doctoral candidates — who wish to be admitted to ICTS as members affiliated with one of its Research Units, pursuant to Art. 8 of the Center's Regulations.

  • Who applies

    Researchers at any career stage, affiliated with a university or research institution.

  • Research Units

    LOGOS · RAISE · CI² · PRISM · FRATERNITAS · OIKOS — or a proposed new unit.

  • You provide

    Your details, the chosen Unit, a short motivation, and a link to your academic profile.

Module B · For Institutions

Request for an agreement.

For universities, research institutes, and other institutions seeking to establish a formal agreement or convention with ICTS. The module is completed by the legal representative or an authorised delegate.

  • Who applies

    The legal representative of the proposing institution, with a designated scientific contact.

  • Type of agreement

    Scientific collaboration, framework convention, faculty exchange, or joint doctoral supervision.

  • You provide

    Institutional details, the linked Research Unit, and the aims and duration of the collaboration.

Open Module B →

Module C · For Members

Activate a laboratory or group.

For current ICTS members, or members of an affiliated Unit, who wish to propose the activation of a new Laboratory, Research Group, or Permanent Seminar within the Center.

  • Who applies

    A current ICTS member, indicating their Unit of affiliation.

  • Structure proposed

    Research Laboratory, Research Group, or Permanent Seminar.

  • You provide

    Objectives and themes, the coordinator and members, and the planned first-year outputs.
