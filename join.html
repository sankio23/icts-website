§ Invitation

The Center grows through invitation.

ICTS welcomes researchers, doctoral candidates, and institutional partners who recognise their own questions in our work — and who are willing to inhabit a slower horizon than most academic environments allow.

For Researchers

A call for fellows.

We invite applications for visiting fellowships, postdoctoral positions, and doctoral co-supervision in any of the Center's six fields of inquiry. Selection is based on the depth of a proposed question, not on a metric.

  • Visiting Fellowship

    Three to twelve months in residence at Loppiano. Open to scholars at all career stages.

  • Postdoctoral

    Two-year positions linked to active inquiries, including the Digital Oath.

  • Doctoral

    Co-supervision with Sophia University Institute and partner institutions.

For Institutions

Partnerships.

The Center collaborates with universities, research institutes, foundations, ecclesial bodies, and civil society organisations on joint inquiries, co-funded chairs, and shared doctoral programmes.

We are particularly interested in partnerships that cross geographical and disciplinary lines — and that can sustain attention over the long horizons our work requires.
