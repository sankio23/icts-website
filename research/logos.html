LOGOS

History of Christianity and Christian Thought

LOGOS investigates the historical development of Christianity and the evolution of Christian thought across cultural, philosophical, and institutional contexts. The unit focuses on how theological and intellectual traditions have shaped social, political, and cultural structures over time, and how they continue to influence contemporary forms of knowledge and collective life.
