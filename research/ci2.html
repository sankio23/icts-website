CI²

Complexity & Information Initiative

CI² investigates how information, cognition, and agency emerge in complex systems. It integrates philosophy of science, physics, and cognitive sciences to develop a unified framework for understanding knowledge as a relational and dynamic phenomenon. Particular attention is devoted to quantum computers and the foundations of quantum computation, as a paradigmatic case of the interplay between physical systems and information processing.
