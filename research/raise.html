RAISE

Responsible Artificial Intelligence and Social Ethics

RAISE addresses the problem of responsibility in AI-driven societies. It investigates how responsibility can be articulated within socio-technical systems where agency is distributed across human and artificial actors, moving beyond purely regulatory or principle-based approaches.
