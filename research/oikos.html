OIKOS

Family, Generations & Communion — International Center for the Study of Family Life (CISVF)

OIKOS investigates family life as a complex, intergenerational, and intercultural reality. Bringing together representatives of international family and youth networks, the unit studies the family from multiple, deeply interconnected perspectives — those of adults and those of younger generations — and articulates how diverse needs, sensibilities, and lived experiences can be harmoniously composed.
