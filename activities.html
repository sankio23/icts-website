§ Continuous Life

Seminars, colloquia, workshops.

A continuous program of intellectual life. Most activities are open to researchers from outside the Center; some are public; a few are deliberately small.

  • Colloquium 12 May 2026

    The Digital Oath — Second International Colloquium

    Three days of plenary lectures and working groups bringing together philosophers, AI researchers, jurists, and policymakers around the second draft of the Oath.

    Loppiano · Sophia University Institute

  • Seminar 08 Apr 2026

    Algorithmic Mediation and Moral Agency

    Monthly seminar of the Philosophy and Ethics of AI field. Presentations followed by an extended common discussion.

    Online · standing seminar

  • Workshop 20 Mar 2026

    Sources of Charism — Editorial Workshop

    Working session for the editorial board of the critical edition. Attendance by invitation.

    Loppiano · closed session

  • Lecture 14 Feb 2026

    Migration as a Theological Category

    Inaugural lecture of the spring term, opening the year of the Atlantic Route field. Open to the public.

    Aula Magna · public lecture

  • Seminar 29 Jan 2026

    Fraternity in International Law

    Joint seminar of the Diplomacy & Fraternity field with the Centre for International Public Law, Leuven.

    Online · joint session

  • Colloquium 12 Dec 2025

    Economy of Communion at Forty

    International colloquium on cooperative and communion-based economic practice, hosted in collaboration with the Universidade de São Paulo.

    São Paulo · USP
